Savory Burger Recipe

Description

Imagine sinking your teeth into a juicy, savory burger crafted to perfection. Start with a thick, seasoned beef patty, gently grilled to a mouthwatering medium-rare. Nestled within a soft, toasted brioche bun, it's layered with crisp, fresh lettuce leaves, slices of ripe tomato, and rings of pungent red onion. A generous smear of creamy mayonnaise and a dollop of tangy ketchup add a delightful richness and sweetness, perfectly complementing the robust flavors of the beef. With each bite, the textures and flavors harmonize, creating a classic, satisfying burger experience that's both timeless and comforting.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Tomatoe
  • Onion
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook the patty.
  2. Toast the bun.
  3. Assemble the burger and enjoy!
